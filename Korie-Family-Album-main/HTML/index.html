About Us

The Korie Family Story The Korie family’s journey is deeply rooted in love, resilience, and shared aspirations. Basil Korie and his wife, Duruofor Obiageri Zita, both originate from the southeastern region of Nigeria—a land rich in culture, strong family values, and home to over 80 percent of the nation’s crude oil and gas reserves. Their union blossomed into a beautiful marriage, blessed with three handsome boys—each a living testament to their parents’ dedication, love, and strength. In 2011, Mr. Basil Korie made the bold decision to move to Canada in search of better opportunities and a brighter future for his young family. A year later, in 2012, his wife Obiageri Zita and their first son joined him, reuniting the family and beginning a new chapter in a foreign land. In 2014, the family relocated to the Northwest Territories, where they have since become a proud part of the northern community. After many years of hesitation, Mr. Korie eventually embraced Yellowknife as home—a decision that marked a turning point in their journey. Despite the challenges that come with migration and resettlement, the Korie family has remained a close-knit and loving unit, building a life rooted in purpose, unity, and hope. Their story is a powerful reminder of the strength of family and the dreams that connect hearts across continents.
